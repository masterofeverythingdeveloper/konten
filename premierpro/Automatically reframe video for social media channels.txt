<p style="border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.5; margin: 10px 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word;">Here are some best practice tips for getting the best results with Auto Reframe.</p>
<p style="border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.5; margin: 10px 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word;"><b style="font-weight: bolder; border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box;"><img src="https://helpx.adobe.com/content/dam/help/icons/tip%20(1).png" alt="" style="border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; width: auto; max-width: 100%;" />Filming:&nbsp;&nbsp;</b>Frame your videos with a little more space around the subjects so that you can repurpose the footage later for any medium.</p>
<p style="border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.5; margin: 10px 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word;"><b style="font-weight: bolder; border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box;"><img src="https://helpx.adobe.com/content/dam/help/icons/tip%20(1).png" alt="" style="border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; width: auto; max-width: 100%;" />Using text titles:<span>&nbsp;</span></b>Auto Reframe works best when titles are created using Premiere Pro.&nbsp;</p>
<p style="border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.5; margin: 10px 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word;"><b style="font-weight: bolder; border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box;"><img src="https://helpx.adobe.com/content/dam/help/icons/tip%20(1).png" alt="" style="border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; width: auto; max-width: 100%;" />Using still images:</b><span>&nbsp;</span>Auto Reframe does not work with still images. Reframe still images separately.</p>
<p style="border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 16px; line-height: 1.5; margin: 10px 0px; overflow-wrap: break-word;">&nbsp;<img src="https://helpx.adobe.com/content/dam/help/icons/tip%20(1).png" alt="" style="border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box; width: auto; max-width: 100%;" /><b style="font-weight: bolder; border: 0px; box-sizing: border-box;">Apply the Auto Reframe effect only once:</b>&nbsp; Applying Auto Reframe multiple times (especially with nested clips) can cause unpredictable results - such as black bars on the sides of your videos. If you need to re-apply the Auto Reframe effect, make sure you apply it on the original clip.</p>
