<h1>Animated GIF</h1>
The Animated GIF function of PhotoScape allows you to create an animated gif slideshow of your pictures. <br /><br /><img src="http://img.photoscape.org/v005/animated_gif/animated_gif-1.jpg" />
<h2>Change Display Time</h2>
Adjust the duration of time that each photo is displayed by changing the Display Time. <br />Increasing the number increases how long a photo is displayed before switching to the next one. <br /><br />Click 1 - <img src="http://img.photoscape.org/v005/animated_gif/change_time-1.jpg" /> <br /><br />Click 2 - <img src="http://img.photoscape.org/v005/animated_gif/change_time-2.jpg" style="margin-left: 15px;" /> <br /><hr />
