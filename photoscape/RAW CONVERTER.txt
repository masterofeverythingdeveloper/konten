<h1 id="raw">RAW Converter</h1>
The RAW converter function of PhotoScape allows you to process a RAW photo (.CR2, .CRW, .NEF, .NRW are the most common format of these) taken by a DSLR camera into a JPG file. <br /><br /><img src="http://img.photoscape.org/v005/intro/raw-1.jpg" /> <br /><br />
<h2>To Add a RAW file to the RAW Converter Queue</h2>
<table>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td style="width: 400px;">1. Click on Add. You may select more than one file at a time.</td>
<td><img src="http://img.photoscape.org/v005/intro/raw-2.jpg" /></td>
<td></td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
<h2>To Remove a RAW file from RAW Converter Queue</h2>
1. Select the file you wish to remove. <br />2. Click on Delete. <br />Note: The Delete All button will remove all files in queue. <br />
<h2>To Adjust RAW Conversion Options</h2>
1. Select one of the options from the Conversion Options section. <br /><br />
