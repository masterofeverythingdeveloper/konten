<h3>Children</h3>
<p>The Child layout has very few and less complex properties compared to the parent! These properties are essentially alignment and positioning controls (based on the Parent direction).</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Alignment</strong>&nbsp;- align objects along one axis</li>
<li><strong>Absolute position</strong>&nbsp;- easily position items within auto layout frames using precise controls.</li>
<li><strong>Resizing -</strong>&nbsp;Lets you have a dynamic or fixed width</li>
</ol>
