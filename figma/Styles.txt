<h2>Styles</h2>
<p>In Figma, most of the foundation elements are set in the <strong>Styles</strong>, which contains <strong>Colors</strong> (including gradients and images), <strong>Text,</strong> and even <strong>Effects</strong>. You can make them readily available as a Library. As you design, you can set these styles in your Inspector, for things like <strong>Text</strong>, <strong>Fill</strong>, <strong>Stroke,</strong> and <strong>Effects</strong>.</p>
