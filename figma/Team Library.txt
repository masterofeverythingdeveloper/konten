<p>Imagine Abstract, Google Docs and Sketch in a single tool. Imagine a more cohesive and smoother experience. That&rsquo;s Figma. You can enable and disable these libraries by clicking the <strong>ON/OFF</strong> toggle anytime you want.</p>
