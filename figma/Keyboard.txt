<h3>Keyboard Shortcuts</h3>
<p>The shortcuts in Figma are very similar to Sketch. Some key differences that are quite important to know:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Command + Option + G</strong>&nbsp;creates a Frame for selected elements. A Frame is like an Artboard.</li>
<li><strong>Control + G</strong>&nbsp;to enable / disable Grid.</li>
<li><strong>K</strong>&nbsp;for Scale, which allows you to scale elements on the fly.</li>
<li><strong>C</strong>&nbsp;for Commenting. Comments are embedded in Figma directly instead of having to go through a different space.</li>
</ul>
<p>You can open the Figma Keyboard Shortcuts by clicking the <strong>Menu</strong> bar, then going to <strong>Help and Account</strong> &gt; <strong>Keyboard Shortcuts</strong> or you can press a shortcut <strong>Ctrl+Shift+?</strong> on your keyboard.</p>
