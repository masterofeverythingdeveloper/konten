<h2>Alignment</h2>
<p>The top of Properties panel contains various alignment options. Provided below is the list of all the options as well as their keyboard shortcuts:</p>
<ul>
<li>Align Left (Alt + A)</li>
<li>Align Horizontal Centers (Alt + H)</li>
<li>Align Right (Alt + D)</li>
<li>Align Top (Alt + W)</li>
<li>Align Vertical Centers (Alt + V)</li>
<li>Align Bottom (Alt + S)</li>
</ul>
